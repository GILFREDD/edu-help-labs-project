Verification

 Mobile & E-mail

An account gets activated once the user verifies both an e-mail address and mobile number. We send an e-mail verification link to your given e-mail and an OTP ( One Time Password ) to the given mobile number. Verifying both e-mail and mobile number adds more security to your account.

We highly recommend you provide an accurate email address so that you're not locked out of your account if you forget your mobile number or can't receive the OTP.

 Using one WhatsApp account on multiple phones, or with multiple phone numbers

Your eduHelp account can only be verified with one number on one device. If you have a dual SIM phone, please note that you still must choose one number to verify with eduHelp. There is no option to have a eduHelp account with two phone numbers.

If you attempt to frequently switch your eduHelp account between different devices, at a certain point, you may be blocked from re-verifying your account. Please do not repeatedly switch between different devices and numbers.
